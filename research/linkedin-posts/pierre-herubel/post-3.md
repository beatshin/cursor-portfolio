Post Content:
We built 30 content systems in 6 months with Content Path.
These 5 pillars turn content into revenue:

1. Create content for passive buyers
Some businesses are not ready to buy and do not want to be in your linear sales sequence.
The best scenario is to build top-of-mind awareness for these buyers through content marketing.
- Develop clear content pillars
- Publish consistently across time
- Vary formats and channels

2. Build a solid intent signals gathering system
Attribution matters, but intent signals are more important in this playbook.
You need to constantly identify prospects with above-average intent.
- Track content engagement across channels
- Score prospects based on signals and frequency
- Centralize signals in one CRM or system

3. Use ads to amplify what already works
Ads will not create demand out of the blue, but they can expand reach and reinforce awareness.
- Promote top-performing content
- Retarget engaged and high-intent audiences
- Focus on repetition, not aggressive messages

4. Get ready to capture demand from active buyers
When buyers are ready, they expect fast responses across channels.
If you take too long to reply to inquiries, you lose opportunities.
- Optimized social media profiles
- Dynamic forms to segment leads
- Soft captures like webinars or guides

5. Run warm outreach for potentially active buyers
Some ready-to-buy prospects who like your brand may never contact you directly.
They may be busy, forget, or assume you will reach out first.
- Build a social selling routine
- Create warm email sequences
- ABM for larger accounts

AI is an enabler across the full playbook.

PS: We have 2 spots left in Q2 2026 to install this playbook.
