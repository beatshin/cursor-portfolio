Post Content:
A lot of B2B businesses neglect distribution in 2026:
- Write a long whitepaper, without social repurposing
- Record a long webinar, without clipping it
- Prioritize long-form, not short-form

The problem is obvious here:
Without proper distribution, content can't deliver strong ROI.

So if they want to win, they'll need to reverse-engineer their content engine:
- Build a distribution strategy that starts from where the audience is
- Think "social-first" when ideating a new content campaign
- Focus 80% of content creation on zero-click content

And this last keyword, "zero-click content" is crucial.
Your audience doesn't want to go on a new platform to consume educative content.
They prefer to consume it right where they are, right now.

Plus, it needs to be unique, rich in insights, actionable, and opinionated.
That's why you need a strong zero-click content engine:
- Short snackable content
- Accessible directly in the feed
- Educational and actionable for the audience

That's what we're building with Content Path.
